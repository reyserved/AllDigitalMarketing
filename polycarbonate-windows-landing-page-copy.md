*Advanced coating and laminating processes*

**Laminating**
- *Bullet-Resistant Multilayer Windows*
- PVB interlayer technology that absorbs impact energy. Ideal for military, security, and high-risk applications.

**Hard Coating**
- *Application-Specific Optical Coatings*
- Scratch-resistant, anti-fog, anti-static, anti-glare, UV-resistant, and chemical-resistant coatings tailored to your specs.

**Screen Printing**
- *Custom Blackout (Frit) Patterns*
- Unlimited frit patterns on sheets up to 5' x 12'. Multi-color inks compatible with silicone hard coats.
