# Individual and Family Wealth Management

Wealth decisions are personal, and every family has a different vision for what comes next. Moran Wealth Management provides fiduciary guidance and personalized planning to help individuals and families in Naples, Florida build, protect, and pass on wealth with clarity and confidence.

## Personalized Wealth Strategy for Every Season of Life
Your priorities evolve over time. We build a comprehensive plan that aligns your investment strategy with your lifestyle, values, and long-term goals. Our approach balances growth and protection while keeping your family aligned on the big picture.

## Holistic Financial Planning
True wealth management extends beyond investments. We take a coordinated view of your entire financial life so decisions stay aligned across cash flow, tax strategy, risk management, and legacy planning.

- Goal and lifestyle planning
- Risk tolerance and liquidity analysis
- Cash flow and savings strategy
- Coordination with your CPA and attorney

## Investment Strategy Built Around Your Priorities
Our in-house portfolio management team uses research-driven strategies tailored to your objectives. With access to 35+ proprietary investment strategies and Separately Managed Accounts (SMAs), we provide transparency and flexibility while keeping your portfolio aligned with your goals.

## Tax-Aware Investing and Ongoing Oversight
Tax efficiency can have a meaningful impact on long-term outcomes. We integrate tax-aware investment decisions and consistent portfolio monitoring to help maintain alignment as markets and life change.

- Asset location and tax-aware rebalancing
- Loss-harvesting considerations and distribution planning
- Ongoing performance review and adjustments

## Retirement Income and Distribution Planning
Retirement planning is not just about saving, it is about building reliable income. We design distribution strategies that account for taxes, market conditions, and changing priorities over time.

- Social Security and IRA optimization
- Tax-aware withdrawal sequencing
- Income planning designed for longevity

## Education Planning and Family Milestones
From college funding to major life events, we help you plan for the moments that matter most.

- 529 plans and education funding strategies
- Financial planning for growing families
- Coordination of gift strategies and family support

## Life Transitions and Planning Support
Major life changes can create financial complexity. We help you adjust your plan through milestones such as career changes, inheritance, relocation, or the transition into retirement, keeping priorities and risk aligned.

## Estate and Legacy Planning Coordination
Your legacy deserves thoughtful structure. We collaborate with your legal team to help ensure your estate plan reflects your intent and supports the people and causes you care about.

- Trust and estate planning coordination
- Multi-generational wealth considerations
- Succession and beneficiary planning

## Philanthropy and Values-Based Giving
Generosity can be a meaningful part of your legacy. We help structure charitable strategies that align with your values while supporting tax efficiency.

- Donor-advised funds and charitable trusts
- Planned giving strategies
- Long-term philanthropic planning

## Risk Management and Family Protection
We evaluate insurance and risk exposures to help protect the plan you have built. This includes reviewing coverage and coordinating with your advisors to keep protection aligned with your goals.

## Family Governance and Communication
Wealth can be a unifying tool when expectations are clear. We help families establish communication frameworks that support transparency and shared decision-making across generations.

## Our Process
We deliver a clear, collaborative experience designed to keep your plan current and aligned.

- Discovery and goal alignment during a complimentary consultation
- Portfolio audit and proposal development
- Investment proposal presentation
- Onboarding and implementation
- Ongoing strategy oversight and proactive reviews

## Frequently Asked Questions
**What is included in individual and family wealth management?**
Our services include comprehensive financial planning, investment management, retirement income strategy, education planning, and coordination of estate and tax planning with your advisors.

**How often do you review my plan and portfolio?**
We provide ongoing oversight and schedule regular reviews, with additional meetings as your goals or circumstances change.

**Do you provide tax or estate planning?**
We do not provide legal or tax advice, but we coordinate closely with your CPA and attorney to align your investment and planning strategy.

**Can you help with college funding?**
Yes. We help evaluate 529 plans and education funding strategies that fit your broader financial plan.

**How do you approach charitable giving?**
We help structure giving strategies such as donor-advised funds or trusts in coordination with your tax and legal advisors.

**Can you provide a second opinion on my current portfolio?**
Yes. We can review your current allocation, risk exposure, and strategy alignment as part of a broader planning conversation.

**What makes Moran Wealth Management different?**
We combine fiduciary guidance, in-house portfolio management, and a collaborative advisory approach to deliver personalized strategies that reflect your goals and values.
