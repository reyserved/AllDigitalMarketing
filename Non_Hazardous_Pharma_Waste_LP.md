### Educational Section (The Problem)
**Headline:** Is Your Pharmaceutical Waste Really "Non-Hazardous"?
**Body Copy:**
Many organizations struggle to classify non-RCRA pharmaceutical waste. While not classified as "hazardous" under federal RCRA standards, these materials—like expired OTC medications, specific manufacturing byproducts, and non-controlled substances—still pose environmental risks if landfilled.

LJP Waste Solutions takes the guesswork out of compliance. We audit your waste streams to ensure proper classification and provide a secure chain of custody from your facility to our waste-to-energy partners.
