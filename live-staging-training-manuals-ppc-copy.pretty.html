Printed Training Materials FAQs

Is there a minimum or maximum quantity you typically print?

Most customers print at least a few hundred booklets annually, and some print tens of thousands across multiple titles. We plan around annual demand and best cost-per-piece, not just one run.

Can you deliver printed course materials to individual learners?

Yes. We support any mix of bulk shipments and direct-to-participant delivery for in-person, hybrid, and virtual training programs.

Can you help with participant kits and premium items?

Absolutely. We can assemble kits with printed materials plus items like pens, sticky notes, certificates, and branded giveaways.

Can we send you our own materials for kit assembly?

Yes. You can ship branded or printed inserts to our team, and we will include them during fulfillment.

Can you warehouse and ship materials as classes run throughout the year?

Yes. We provide inventory management and on-demand shipping so your training teams stay stocked without overprinting.

Where should we send an RFP for training manual printing?

Send your proposal to JustAsk@omnipress.com and our team will follow up promptly.
