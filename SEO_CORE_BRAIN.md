* **Contextual Application:** You must only apply "Best Practices" to *relevant* tasks. Do not apply a "SaaS Pricing Page" best practice to a "Family Law Blog" post.

## **4. Specialized Frameworks**

### **A. Family Law Paid Landing Page Protocol**

*(Refined from Feedback Application Analysis - 2026-01-28)*

**1. The "Strategic Fighter" Positioning (Tone & Identity)**
*   **Concept:** Move beyond generic "aggressive" or "compassionate" labels. Position as **"Calm, Dangerous Competence."**
*   **Key Phrasing:** "Fight Smart," "Courtroom Readiness," "Firm when needed, practical always."
*   **Goal:** Signal that we *can* destroy the opposition, but we choose to *negotiate from strength* to save the client money/stress.

**2. The "Segmented Hero" Architecture**
*   **Headline Formula:** [Niche Location] + [Identity: Strategic Fighters] + [Benefit: Protect Future].
    *   *Example:* "Georgia Family Law Attorneys Who Fight Smart for Your Family and Future."
*   **Instant Path Selection (Critical):** Immediately below the main CTA, place a **"Choose Your Issue" button row** (Divorce | Custody | Support | etc.).
    *   *Function:* These must anchor-scroll to specific content blocks, solving the "Broad Ad vs. Specific Intent" problem without requiring 7 separate pages.

**3. Practice Area "Mini-Story" Modules**
*Do not just list services. Transform every Practice Area block into a 4-part conversion unit:*
1.  **The Stakes (Empathy + Risk):** ONE sentence on what is lost if this goes wrong (e.g., "Terms can affect you for years").
2.  **The Action (How We Help):** Concrete deliverables (e.g., "Value assets," "Draft parenting plans").
3.  **The Differentiation (Why Us):** A "Reason-Why" claim (e.g., "We prepare for litigation so fairness is the only option").
4.  **Micro-CTA:** Specific to the topic (e.g., "Talk to a lawyer about Custody").

**4. Scientific Advertising & Conversion Mechanics**
*   **The "Offer Stack" Upgrade:**
    *   *Generic:* "Free Consultation."
    *   *Optimized:* "Confidential Case Evaluation." (Adds weight/value).
*   **Friction Reduction:** Place these **Trust Signals** explicitly near every primary CTA:
    *   "Confidential" (Crucial for Family Law).
    *   "No Obligation."
    *   "We respond within [X] hours" (Speed assurance).
*   **Reason-Why Copy:** Every claim of "Quality" must follow with "Because [Reason]."
    *   *Example:* "We handle uncontested divorces *with strict oversight* because a single paperwork error can cost thousands later."

**5. Trust & Authority Injection**
*   **Proof Bar:** Place distinct proof elements (Star Ratings, "Avvo Rated," Case Counts) **Above the Fold**.
*   **Disclaimer:** "Prior results do not guarantee similar outcomes" (Mandatory for compliance, but builds honesty trust).

**6. Hub vs. Spoke Decision (Critical Pre-Writing Step)**
Before writing any content, determine the lander type:

| Type | Use Case | Content Depth | Example Keyword Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hub Lander** | Broad campaigns, brand awareness, remarketing. | Comprehensive. Multiple service mentions. | `[family law attorney atlanta]` |
| **Spoke Lander** | Single-keyword ad groups, high-intent traffic. | Laser-focused. One service only. | `[divorce lawyer near me]` |

*   **Hub Landers:** Require Segmentation Row; can include multiple Mini-Story modules.
*   **Spoke Landers:** Remove all non-essential content. H1 must directly match ad group keyword.

**7. Process Simplification Rule**
*   Limit all "Our Process" sections to **3 steps maximum**, framed from the user's perspective:
    1.  **Consult:** "Book a Confidential Consultation."
    2.  **Strategy:** "Get a clear plan and options."
    3.  **Action:** "We execute—negotiation or litigation."
*   Users don't need internal workflow details; they need to see *their* journey.

**8. Compliance Footer (Non-Negotiable for YMYL)**
For any legal, medical, or financial landing page, include:
*   **Disclaimer:** "Prior results do not guarantee similar outcomes. Information on this page is for general purposes only."
*   **Relationship:** "No attorney-client relationship is formed until a written fee agreement is signed."
*   **Location:** "Serving [Service Area]."
