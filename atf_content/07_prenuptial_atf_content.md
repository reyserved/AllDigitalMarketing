### Intro Content

A prenuptial agreement isn't about planning for failure—it's about protecting both partners and having a clear understanding of financial expectations before marriage. In Florida, a properly drafted prenup can define separate property, waive alimony, and protect family businesses or inheritances.

At **Vasquez de Lara Law Group**, we approach prenuptial agreements with sensitivity and strategic foresight. Whether you're bringing significant assets into the marriage, own a business, or simply want financial clarity, we create agreements that are enforceable and fair to both parties.
