### Intro Content

Court orders aren't carved in stone. When there's a substantial, material, and unanticipated change in circumstances—such as job loss, relocation, remarriage, or a child's changing needs—Florida law allows for modification of custody, support, and alimony orders.

At **Vasquez de Lara Law Group**, we help clients on both sides of modification petitions. Whether you need to seek a change or defend against one, we provide strategic representation to achieve the best possible outcome.

### Modification Services

- **Child Support Modifications** — Income changes, job loss
- **Alimony Modifications** — Retirement, remarriage, cohabitation
- **Time-Sharing Modifications** — Custody schedule changes
- **Relocation Cases** — When a parent wants to move
