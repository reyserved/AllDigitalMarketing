### Intro Content

When it comes to your children, nothing matters more than ensuring they have a stable, loving relationship with both parents. Florida uses the term "time-sharing" instead of custody, and courts focus on what arrangement serves the **best interests of the child**.

At **Vasquez de Lara Law Group**, we understand that custody battles are emotionally charged. Whether you're seeking primary custody, fighting for equal time-sharing, or need to modify an existing parenting plan, we advocate fiercely for your parental rights while keeping your children's well-being at the center of every decision.
