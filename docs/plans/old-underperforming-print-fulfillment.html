Fulfillment Pricing

We warehouse and fulfill printed materials that we produce for our customers. Therefore, our standard contract includes the cost of both.

Print and fulfillment pricing is determined by the following:

  • Print quantity
  • Page count
  • Finished size
  • Number of colors
  • Binding type
  • Finishing (folds, perforations, etc.)
  • Order volume and frequency
  • Shipping destination
  • Whether kitting services are also included

As part of our initial discovery process, we will walk you through an in-depth Cost Calculator that includes the details behind the estimate.
