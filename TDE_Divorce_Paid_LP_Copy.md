### [Uncontested Divorce](#uncontested-divorce)
*   **The Stakes:** Even when both parties agree, a single paperwork error can cost you thousands later.
*   **The Action:** We prepare all filings, ensure agreements are enforceable, and guide you to the fastest resolution possible.
*   **The Difference:** The fastest and least expensive way to dissolve a marriage—done right the first time.

### [Contested Divorce](#contested-divorce)
*   **The Stakes:** When you can't agree on assets, debt, custody, or support, the process can drag on for months—or years.
*   **The Action:** We build a litigation-ready case to protect your interests in negotiations and, if necessary, in court.
*   **The Difference:** We prepare for trial so that fair negotiation becomes the only logical option.
*   **Micro-CTA:** [Talk to a Lawyer About Contested Divorce]

### [High-Asset Divorce](#high-asset-divorce)
*   **The Stakes:** Complex investments, multiple properties, business interests, and retirement accounts require forensic-level attention.
*   **The Action:** We work with financial experts to identify, value, and equitably divide high-value marital assets under Georgia law.
*   **The Difference:** We don't leave money on the table—every asset is accounted for and protected.
*   **Micro-CTA:** [Talk to a Lawyer About High-Asset Divorce]

### [LGBTQ+ Divorce](#lgbtq-divorce)
*   **The Stakes:** LGBTQ+ divorces can involve unique legal considerations around property, parental rights, and prior agreements.
*   **The Action:** We provide specialized, compassionate legal support tailored to the unique needs of the LGBTQ+ community.

*   **Micro-CTA:** [Talk to a Lawyer About LGBTQ+ Divorce]

### [Legal Separation](#legal-separation)
