INTERACTIVE MODULE LIBRARY (LOCKED STACK)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Feature 1: Counter + Micro Chart (hero/right card or offer card)
- Free sources:
  - CountUp.js: https://inorganik.github.io/countUp.js/
  - Chart.js: https://www.chartjs.org/
- Pattern:
  - animate only RATE and APR counters
  - place 11-payment chart directly below counters
  - chart model: 10 equal short bars + 1 final tall bar (visual normalization)
  - tooltip shows actual amounts
- Required wrapper classes:
  - js-b59-ppc-counter-module
  - b59-ppc-hero-kpi-card

Feature 2: Qualification Stepper (updated)
- Free source: vanilla JS only
- Pattern:
  - desktop: tabbed step content
  - mobile: accordion step content
  - no Back/Next/Done button row
  - final step contains lender CTA
- Events:
  - qualification_expand (first interaction)
  - cta_talk_lender_click with placement=qualification_stepper

Feature 3: Motion Accent Layer
- Free sources:
  - LottieFiles free animations: https://lottiefiles.com/free-animation
  - Haikei SVG generator: https://haikei.app/
- Rules:
  - subtle only; do not compete with CTA
  - decorative layers behind content only
  - reduced-motion fallback mandatory
