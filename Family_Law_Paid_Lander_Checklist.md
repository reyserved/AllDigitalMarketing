# Family Law Paid Lander Checklist

Use this checklist to validate every Family Law paid landing page before submission.

## 1. Pre-Flight Decisions
- [ ] Hub or Spoke type selected and documented.
- [ ] Primary ad group keyword identified.
- [ ] Service area and location phrasing defined.
- [ ] Target user intent confirmed (divorce, custody, support, etc.).

## 2. Hero and Message Match
- [ ] H1 matches the ad keyword theme.
- [ ] Hero identity reflects "fight smart" positioning.
- [ ] Primary CTA is specific (e.g., "Confidential Case Evaluation").
- [ ] Proof bar with at least 3 proof points appears above the fold.

## 3. Segmentation (Hub Only)
- [ ] Segmentation row present with anchor links for key services.
- [ ] Each button scrolls to a matching content block.

## 4. Practice Area Modules
- [ ] Each service block follows the Stakes, Action, Differentiation, Micro-CTA format.
- [ ] No generic service lists without a conversion narrative.

## 5. Trust and Friction Reduction
- [ ] "Confidential" and "No Obligation" appear near primary CTAs.
- [ ] Response-time promise included if available.
- [ ] Reason-why statements follow any quality claim.

## 6. Process and Flow
- [ ] Process section is 3 steps or less.
- [ ] Steps are user-centric: Consult, Strategy, Action.

## 7. FAQs
- [ ] FAQs are short (2 to 3) or removed for paid traffic.

## 8. Compliance Footer (Required)
- [ ] "Prior results do not guarantee similar outcomes."
- [ ] "No attorney-client relationship until written agreement."
- [ ] "Serving [Service Area]."
